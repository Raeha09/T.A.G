Welcome to The Anonymous Gambler. Inspired by a Gambler aiming to recover and aspiring to motivate others.

The Anonymous Gambler or (TAG) for short. Has been set up and inspired by a Gamblers own life experiences and journey to trying to recover from a gambling addiction. It's been a rocky road and a very tiresome trek but... The Gambler is still here and with plenty of memoirs and tales to tell about their journey along the way to recovery.

The main aim was to create a platform which could be used to talk to fellow or recovering Gamblers about their life experiences. And give those that are willing to share a platform to give feedback on the impact those experiences have had on their life...good or bad!.
